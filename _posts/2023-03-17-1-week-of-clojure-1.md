There are lots of shortcuts worth learning. Below ğŸ‘‡ are the top 4 that I most use so far. I'll continue to post these as I discover them.

- `ctrl`+`enter`: evaluate current form.
- `ctrl`+`alt`+`c`, `enter`: load / reload current file.
- `escape`: remove all evaluated results.
